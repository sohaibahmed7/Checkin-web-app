How Check-In Works

1. Download the App

Get Check-In from the App Store and set up your profile in under 2 minutes.

2. Find Your Neighborhood

Search for your neighborhood and request to join. You'll need an access code from your community moderator.

3. Connect & Stay Safe

Once approved, receive private alerts, chat with neighbors, and help keep your community informed.

About Community Moderators

Each neighborhood is private and managed by trusted moderators who:

  • Control access to the neighborhood group
  • Verify new members through access codes
  • Manage community discussions
  • Ensure neighborhood safety updates stay private
